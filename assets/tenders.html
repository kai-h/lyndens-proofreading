Tenders

Give your tenders loving care!

Quotes and tenders are an integral part of the business world, but they can and do get knocked back on a technicality.

While not necessarily having expertise in your specific area, I am thoroughly trained in the art of spotting anomalies, omissions and errors.

Feel free to test my testimonials.

If your tender document is confusing in its layout, contradicts itself, has holes in it, is not thorough enough, fails to answer a specific point, or errs grammatically, it'll probably get rejected out of hand. 

Send it to me first and your chances of success will increase. Dramatically.

That's my job; that's my passion!
