About Me

Having worked in the printing and publishing industry for over 35 years, I can bring a wealth of experience to your project, focussing on high-quality, accurate and fast proofreading.

Work history:

During 2006, I proofread articles, journals and newsletters for colleges and government departments in Hobart, as well as various scientific journals, and monthly tender documents for a leading Tasmanian security firm. I also proofread for Clemenger Tasmania on a regular basis as a freelance contractor.

Current clients include:
The Federal Group Tasmania
University of Tasmania
Clemenger Tasmania
Hobart College
Department of Health and Human Services
Tasmania's Temptations Holiday Book (6 years in a row)

Four years of full-time proofreading with Curtis, Jones & Brown,
located in Balmain, NSW – arguably one of Australia's top
healthcare advertising agencies (2001-2005)

Seven years of full-time proofreading with Clemenger Tasmania
(1994-2001)

Also full-time proofreading with Penfold Buscombe
(ex. Mercury-Walch), The Type Shop, Photolith, and the
Printing Authority of Tasmania (between 1988 and 1994)

Scope of work:
Annual reports, scientific journals, brochures, fliers,
tender documents, legal contracts, business cards,
letterheads, with compliments slips, detail aids and
prescribing information (healthcare industry),
newspaper advertisements etc.

I would be interested in offering my skills to you and I guarantee to enhance anything you would like to produce in written form.

Please do not hesitate to contact me for further information.
